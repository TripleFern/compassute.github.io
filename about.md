Compassute is a portfolio site dedicated to showcasing a blend of **AI-generated images** and **photography**.

The project aims to present a curated collection of works, emphasizing the unique aesthetic qualities of both mediums.

### AI Generated Images
We utilize various tools such as Lumalabs Dream Machine, FLUX, and SDXL to create landscapes and artistic imagery.

### Photography
A collection of real-world photography, focusing on capturing moments with artistic intent.
